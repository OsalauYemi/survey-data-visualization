
Survey Data Visualization Project

Overview:
This project analyzes customer survey data to extract key insights about satisfaction levels, service quality, and preferences. The dataset includes Likert scale responses, multiple-choice feedback, and open-ended responses.

Visuals:
- Bar Chart: Satisfaction scores across 5 categories
- Pie Chart: Service usage distribution
- Heatmap: Likert scale summary (Strongly Agree to Strongly Disagree)

Key Insights:
- Most customers are satisfied with customer support and pricing
- A majority prefer online over in-person services
- Feature requests suggest a growing need for automation

Tools Used:
- Power BI
- Excel
- Data Cleaning in Sheets
