
Survey Data Visualization Project

Overview:
This project visualizes customer survey data to reveal insights on satisfaction levels and service channel preferences. Tools used include Excel, Python (matplotlib), and Power BI-style visual storytelling.

Key Charts:
- Satisfaction Bar Chart: Shows customer ratings across 5 key service areas
- Channel Pie Chart: Illustrates the breakdown of preferred support channels

Insights:
- Customer Support ranks highest in satisfaction
- Online services are the preferred support channel (55%)
